La Plantation d'Albion

Hôtel La Plantation is located in Langon and has a bar. Free WiFi access is available.Each room here will provide you with a TV and a fan. There is a traditional restaurant 500 m away.Sauterne is a 10-minute drive from the hotel, Saint-Emilion is a 40-minute drive away, and guests can enjoy a public indoor swimming pool 500 m away. The property offers free parking.
